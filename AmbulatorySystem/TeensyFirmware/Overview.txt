Here you will find firmware for Teensy 3.6.  Compile and upload to Teensy 3.6 board using Arduino IDE
The main file is Intan_ambulatory_20181216.ino.  Opening it in Arduino should open a bunch of tabs with other .ino and .h files
Note: you will also need Bill Greimans' sdfat library before compiling: https://github.com/greiman/SdFat
